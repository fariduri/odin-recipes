The Secret to Make The Perfect Little Girls

Description

Sugar, spice, everything nice and an accidental dose of Chemical X. These are the ingridients to make the classic 90's hit TV show, The Powerpuff Girls. With a slight tweak to the ingridients, soon you will become a father to three super powered little girls.

But before you start, you need to prepare three pieces of Krabby Patty beforehand.

Ingridients

  • 6 cups of water
  • 600g of Sugar
  • 600g of Spice
  • 600g of items infused with happy memories before you become a jaded and cynical adult
  • A can of Whoopass
  • 3 pieces of Krabby Patty

Steps

  1. First make sure you have 3 pieces of Krabby Patty prepared. You can find how to make them on this page.
  2. Blend or finely chop all of items infused with happy memories.
  3. Mix the sugar, spice and the blended happy items together and boil them together using 6 cups of water for 45 minutes.
  4. Soak the three Krabby Patty inside the mixture for 10 minutes.
  5. Take them out and customize each krabby patty with sugar, spice and the blended happy items to your liking.
  6. Put an accidental dose of Whoopass to all the Krabby Patty and Viola, you are finally obligated to pay for child care.
