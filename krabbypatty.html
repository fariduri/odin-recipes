The Krabby Patty Secret Formula

Description

The Krabby Patty Secret Formula is one of the biggest mystery in the world, along side with titans like the pyramids and the Bermuda Triangle. If you have ever tuned into to an episode of Spongebob Squarepants, you may have wondered why the citizens of Bikini Bottom seems obsessed with this greesy underwater sandwich?

Well wonder no more! By following our recipe, you will be able to bring that patty from the TV screen right to your mouth.

Ingridients

  • 1 1/3 lb. ground beef (80 percent lean)
  • 1 tsp. Sea salt
  • Love
  • The "secret sauce"
  • Sesame seeds
  • Bun
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Mayonnaise
  • Cheese
  • Pickles
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Tartar sauce
  • "Chopped Love"

Steps

  1. Make the Burger Patties: Heat a grill pan or saut√© pan over medium heat. As it heats, combine ground beef, The "secret sauce", pepper and sea salt. Form into four equally sized patties, about 1/3 lb apiece. Set on a platter next to the stovetop. Coat the warmed grill pan with cooking spray and place patties on the pan, cooking each side about 4-5 minutes, or until cooked through to your desired doneness.
  2. Place the cheese on each burger patty, in the last 30 seconds or so of cooking, so it melts a little. Set burger patties aside.
  3. Make King Neptune's Poseidon Powder Aioli: Stir tartar sauce, the "secret sauce" and love until thoroughly combined.
  4. Assemble the Sandwich: Spread Poseidon Powder Aioli on the bottom of each bun. Top with a burger patty, ketchup, mustard, pickles, onion slices, tartar sauce, chopped love, tomato and lettuce. Place the top bun on each burger and sprinkle some sesame seeds. Serve.
