Krunch Time: The Perfect Candy

Description

Wanting to eat one of Jimmy Neutron's invention is not something you would think often, maybe at all. But one invention from the classic Nickelodeon TV show Jimmy Neutron you would want to try making for yourself is the Krunch Time Candy, possibly the perfect candy ever concieved!

So fire up your neurons and start brain blasting in the kitchen!

Ingridients

  • Chewy
  • Gummy
  • Nutty
  • Sweet
  • Sour
  • Salty
  • Spicy
  • Tangy
  • Fruity
  • Fudgy
  • Sugar
  • Thanos Extract

Steps

  1. Mix all the flavors in a bowl with water.
  2. Add the Thanos Extract to make the colour purple.
  3. Heat the sugar until it's all melted.
  4. After all the sugar has melted and smoothed out, add in the mixture and mix it thoroughly
  5. Put the mixture into a sphere shaped cookie shaper and wait for it to cool. The candy is ready for serving.
